## What it does

- Accepts image, reel, long-video, and movie/platform links.
- Downloads direct public image/audio/video files through the local proxy in original source quality.
- Previews direct image/video/audio links.
- Exports short video segments from direct video links with browser `MediaRecorder`.
- Detects protected platforms and routes users to the original service or legal movie access search instead of bypassing DRM or platform controls.
